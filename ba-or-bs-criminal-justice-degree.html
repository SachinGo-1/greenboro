Prepare for a Purpose-Driven Career in Criminal Justice

Step into a career that champions justice and public safety with Greensboro College’s online BA/BS in Criminal Justice. Through a curriculum that delves into the complexities of the justice system, from forensic analysis to court procedures, this program equips you with the knowledge and practical skills needed to make a real difference in your community.

Ready to take the next step?

Key Features of Greensboro College’s BA/BS in Criminal Justice

The online program in Criminal Justice equips students with a holistic understanding of the justice system, enriched by interdisciplinary perspectives and real-world experience. Through a curriculum that emphasizes critical thinking, cultural awareness, and hands-on application, graduates are prepared to lead and make meaningful contributions in the criminal justice field.

Comprehensive Understanding of the Criminal Justice System

Explore the core components of the U.S. criminal justice system, from law enforcement to corrections and the courts, across local, state, and federal levels. This foundational knowledge empowers graduates to navigate and impact the justice system at multiple levels.

Cultural Competency and Diversity Awareness

With a focus on cultural diversity within the justice system, this program prepares you to work effectively in diverse environments and address systemic issues. Graduates emerge with the empathy and insight needed to champion inclusivity and justice in their careers.

Hands-On Internship for Real-World Insight

Gain practical experience through a required internship that provides you with valuable practical exposure to the criminal justice system, helping you develop professional connections and apply your academic knowledge in real-world contexts.

Small Classes and Personalized Attention

With small class sizes, Greensboro College offers a learning environment that prioritizes your success. Students benefit from individualized mentorship and close faculty support, ensuring guidance tailored to their academic and professional goals.

Why Get a BA/BS in Criminal Justice?

Deeper Understanding of the Justice System

A bachelor’s degree in Criminal Justice provides graduates with a comprehensive view of law enforcement, the judicial process, and corrections. This foundational knowledge prepares students to analyze policies, understand societal impacts, and contribute meaningfully within the justice field.

Critical and Ethical Thinking for Complex Challenges

Criminal Justice education emphasizes critical thinking, analytical skills, and ethical reasoning—essential abilities for navigating complex issues. Graduates develop problem-solving skills and ethical judgment, empowering them to address real-world challenges in diverse criminal justice roles.

Flexible Career Paths and Advancement Potential

With a Criminal Justice degree, graduates are prepared for varied career paths, including law enforcement, victim advocacy, corrections, and legal assistance. This degree also lays the groundwork for advanced studies in law, criminal justice, and related fields, supporting long-term career growth.

Impactful Contributions to Society

Graduates with a Criminal Justice background are well-positioned to promote justice, equity, and public safety in their communities. This degree equips them with the knowledge and skills needed to make a positive societal impact through meaningful and purposeful work.
